Susan Barlin - President / CEO

Welcome to Forever Rich’s G5 Bayanihan Online Shopping Spirit (G5 BOSS) Program. Filipinos are the most hardworking and tenacious people in the world. This explains why we, under any circumstances, thrive as individuals in being the most resourceful and creative people when it comes to earning a living.

However, it has always been a challenge for Filipinos to cope with the cost of living and inflation versus the ability to earn more than barely necessary. Thus, 67% of the Filipinos are still under the category of POOR!

My humble beginning and global exposure and success are the factors that had roused me to inspire the Filipinos to ignite a revolution to fight poverty. I honestly believe that poverty is the worst kind of violence and is the root of our miseries. Once we alleviate this, the best years of our lives will begin.

Hence, the birth of G5 BOSS program, we’ve kept it simple to understand, affordable to join and easy to duplicate. Purchasing a package of P1500 worth of products makes one a member and can invite 5 other members to join his/her team of 5 under G5 BOSS program. Yes, P1500 and 5 friends in your team will be the beginning a life changing journey where poverty will be a memory. Almost too good to be true, but just like People’s Power when we free our country from tyranny, G5 will actually has the same concept. Together we will make another history.

We offer a wide range of products and it is our goal to make G5 Platform to be like an Online Shopping Mall of products like Personal Care, Beauty, Health, Household and Office, Travel and Real Estate amongst many others. We shall start small and we intend to grow from this humble beginning just like the story of my life.

Helping people is my passion and seeing people succeed feeds my soul. If I have inspired you to be passionate in achieving your dreams and sharing your victory with your friends and family, then my goal has come to fruition. As the saying goes, “You can only lead the horse to water but you can’t make the horse drink.” Welcome to our fertile waters. Now, the decision is yours to capture this opportunity to make your aspirations happen and be a part of this legacy.
